LKMC.Prefactor.estimatePrefactor(initialSumLogEVs, saddleSumLogEVs)[source]¶

Estimates prefactor by using pre-calculated eigenvalues from the initial and saddle states.

Eigenvalues are expressed as such: initialSumLogEVs = log(ev1) + log(ev2) + .. log(evN)
saddleSumLogEVs = log(ev2) + log(ev3) + .. log(evN)
