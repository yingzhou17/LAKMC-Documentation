LKMC.NEB module¶

Nudged elastic band (NEB) module.

Implements the NEB method: http://theory.cm.utexas.edu/henkelman/research/saddle/neb/ Including: climbing image: http://dx.doi.org/10.1063/1.1329672

improved tangent estimate: http://dx.doi.org/10.1063/1.1323224
