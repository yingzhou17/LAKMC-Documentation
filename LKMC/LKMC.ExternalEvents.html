LKMC.ExternalEvents module¶

ExternatEvents.py is a module to handle the inclusion of special events in LKMC using software such as LBOMD or LAMMPS. For a given mean rate of occurrence, an equivalent “energy barrier” is calculated, such that a possible event might be selected along side ordinary KMC moves.

getVelocityFromKE(energy, direction=None, mass=None, sym=None)[source]¶

Returns the velocity for the given kinetic energy and species.

Parameters:

energy : float

The kinetic energy of the atom in eV.

direction : numpy.ndarray, optional

A vector of length 3 defining the direction of the atom. If specified the returned value will also be a vector of length 3 containing the components of velocity, otherwise the magnitude of the velocity is returned

sym : str[2], optional

The symbol of the species, eg. ‘H_‘ or “Fe”. One of sym or mass must be specified. Mass is used if both are given.

mass : float, optional

The total mass of the atom(s) you are calculating the velocity for. One of sym or mass must be specified. Mass is used if both are given.
Returns:

velocity : float or numpy.ndarray

Depending on the value of direction this is the magnitude of the velocity of a vector containing the components of velocity. The units depend on the MD library. For LBOMD it will be Å/fs, for LAMMPS it will be Å/ps (assuming metal units). Support for other LAMMPS units can also be added.
Raises:

LammpsUnitsNotSupported

If the value of the lammpsUnits parameter is not supported.
