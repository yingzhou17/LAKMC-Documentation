LKMC.Profiling module¶

Profiling module.

Interfaces with hotshot module to obtain profiling data.

Author: Chris Scott

LKMC.Profiling.profileMethod(method, args, kwargs, statsFile=None, printStats=1)[source]¶

Profile one run of ‘method’.

‘method’ should be the callable to be profiled. ‘args’ should be a tuple of positional args to ‘method’. ‘kwargs’ should be a dict of key word args to ‘method’.
